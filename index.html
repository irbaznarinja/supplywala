    About SupplyWala

    15 years of supply experience. Now online, for everyone in Karachi.

    Who We Are

    My name is Shahbaz Narinja. I started SupplyWala in May 2026, but I have been in this supply business for over 15 years.

    Before SupplyWala, I ran Younus and Sons, where I supplied hospitals and organizations across Karachi with everything from surgical items and medical equipment to hardware, janitorial supplies, furniture, tools, gloves, and industrial products. If a facility needed it, we found it and delivered it.

    What pushed me to start SupplyWala was simple. I kept seeing the same products I was sourcing locally being sold on websites for 30 to 50 percent more. That extra amount had nothing to do with quality. It was just the cost of going through too many layers before reaching the buyer. I wanted to cut that out and bring those same products directly to people at honest local prices, without them having to leave their office or facility.

    SupplyWala is for anyone in Karachi who wants the convenience of ordering online but at prices that actually make sense.

    What We Supply

    We supply hospitals, clinics, offices, factories, and organizations across Karachi. Our catalog covers 100+ products across medical, surgical, cleaning, hardware, industrial, furniture, tools, and janitorial categories. We are adding more every week.

    What Makes Us Different

    We are especially known for finding items that no other supplier in Karachi can source. Not a specific type of item, literally anything. A facility once needed an eye shower. Another needed a specific cell that no supplier in the city could locate. We found both and delivered. If you have been told something is not available in Karachi, contact us before giving up.

    Where We Are

    Lohar Street, Ranchore Lines, Karachi. We deliver across all major areas of Karachi within 2 to 4 working days.
